SOFTWARE AND HARDWARE REQUIREMENTS
Software Requirements:

Operating System: Windows/Linux/macOS

Programming Language: Python 3.8+

Libraries: TensorFlow, Keras, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn

Tools: Jupyter Notebook, Google Colab (for GPU access)

Hardware Requirements:

Processor: Intel Core i5/i7 or equivalent

RAM: Minimum 8 GB (16 GB recommended)

GPU: NVIDIA GPU with CUDA support (e.g., GTX 1050 Ti or higher)

Storage: At least 50 GB free space for datasets and models

Modern deep learning models require substantial computational resources, especially during training. Cloud platforms like Google Colab and Kaggle Kernels offer free access to GPUs, making it feasible to train deep models without local GPU dependency.
